Generation Thailand JSD9

Generation Thailand JSD9 follows a group of diverse individuals in Thailand who embark on an intense online bootcamp to learn software development. The story delves into their personal and professional struggles as they navigate the challenges of coding, imposter syndrome, and the pressures of the tech industry. Through their shared journey, they form unexpected bonds of friendship, discover their strengths, and ultimately face the uncertainty of their future careers.
